- Server Rules -

Yes, it's just a game, but we take player conduct on our servers very seriously. We pride outselve on a fun, relaxed environment to play in and our rules are rigorously enforced with that in mind.

  • Friendly Fire is always ON
  • All activity is logged
  • Abusive language, swearing, racist names, and behaviour are not tolerated
  • Hacks, cheats, and map exploits are all perma-bans
  • Deliverate and retaliatory TA/TKing will result in a time ban
  • Pistol rolling with scoped weapons is not tolerated **
  • Clans are responsible for ALL their members' behaviour

** Pistol rolling is quick-switching your pistol between scoped weapon shots to avoid the reload animation. We consider this a game exploit contrary to the spitiy of fair play. No, we don't care that all the 'pros' do it :)
